our story



In 2008, co-founder, Jessica Chiartas, was
living in Washington, DC, working in sales
for Schering-Plough pharmaceuticals.
During a one month, crash-course training
in anatomy and physiology, her sense of
wonder and curiosity for the sciences was
reignited. She began reading academic
texts and papers to better understand the
products she was selling.

It didn’t take long to realize that there was
a vast disconnect between the scientific
literature and the marketing materials she
had been trained on. She began reading
papers from other fields of interest like
agriculture and nutrition, as well. It
became quickly apparant that pretty
much across the board, science had a
major failure to communicate!

But where to start? While traveling over-
seas, she learned about WWOOFing, a
program that provides free room and
board to anyone willing to get their
hands dirty working on farm. It didn’t take
very long, digging in the dirt, for her to
recognize soil as the connector of all
things -- including many of the major
issues she had now grown so passionate
about.

She was admitted to the University of Cal-
ifornia, Davis for a PhD in Soils & Biogeo-
chemistry in 2014 -- tthe same year that
the California state Healthy Soils Initiative
began. At the first public meeting, she
witnessed the power of storytelling to
captivate an audience and distill down a
powerful message, but was also confront-
ed with concerns from her academic
peers about what they perceived as hy-
perbole; a failure to communicate
nuance and uncertainty regarding some-
thing as dynamic and diverse as soil.

Recognizing the importance of framing
rather than spinning a story and the im-
portance of finding clever, creative ways
to communicate nuance and uncertainty,
while inspiring curiousity and a sense of
wonder for the natural world. What start-
ed as a graduate student seminar to dis-
cuss current issues in science communi-
cation, quickly evolved into a public rela-
tions campaign to raise awareness about
the value and importance of soil and ulti-
mately, has culminated in the production
of this website and the tools housed
within it! We hope you enjoy!
