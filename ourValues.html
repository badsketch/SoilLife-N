our values



the dirty truth - with misinformation on the rise, we
value accuracy, scientific rigor, and integrity in ev-
erything we do. truth is power.

soilutions - there is enough fear based media
out there. we value positivity, optimism and em-
powering people with solutions, rather than fo-
cusing on the problems. instead of fighting
against a broken system, we value the creation
of new models that make old paradigms obso-
lete.

common ground - in an increasingly black and
white world, we value the spectrum of gray that
lies in the middle, as well as the ability to find
alignment amongst disparate groups. far more
unites us than divides us.

aggregation - we value the interconnectedness
of all things. just as fungal threads and plant
roots bring soil particles together to form aggre-
gates, we believe in bringing together consum-
ers, farmers, and businesses with shared values.
strength in numbers.

groundedness - in a cloud-driven world, we
strive to bring people back down-to-earth. we
value balance in our agricultural systems, busi-
nesses, and individual lives, as well as holistic,
place based thinking.

deep roots - while we all need wings to y, we
value investing in deep roots. now, more than
ever, we must build stable, resilient agriculture
and food systems. cultivate you inner and outer
garden.

exploring new depths - space may be the final
frontier, but some argue we know less about the
soil underfoot then the celestial bodies overhead.
we value exploration and inquiry to discover
what lies beneath!

grit - we value the courage and resolve to stand
up to naysayers and fight for whatâ€™s right; the
strength of character to never give up on what
we believe in; and the tenacity to pursue our
vision relentlessly.

organic growth - the journey of 1000 miles
begins with a single step. we value the patience
it takes to usher in real change, as well as the
sensibility to live within our means. progress over
perfection.
